What is the purpose of StockVision?

StockVision is an educational platform designed to help students and researchers understand how machine learning models approach stock price prediction. It is not meant for actual trading.

Where does the data come from?

For this demo, stock data is either fetched from public APIs or uses locally stored sample data. The backend handles data retrieval and preprocessing.
